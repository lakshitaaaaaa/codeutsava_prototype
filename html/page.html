CodeUtsava, organized annually by the Turing Club of Programmers, is a premier event designed to unite passionate coders from across the nation and promote a vibrant coding culture among students. The event features a diverse array of activities, including workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, gaming battles, mic sessions, and more.

The highlight of CodeUtsava is its much-anticipated hackathon. This 28-hour marathon challenges teams of participants from all academic years and regions to develop innovative applications. This year's CodeUtsava offers an impressive prize pool of 33 lakhs, with cash prizes ranging from 1.5 to 2 lakhs.
